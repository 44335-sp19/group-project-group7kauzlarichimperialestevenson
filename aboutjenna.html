About Me

I am currently a student at Northwest Missouri State University pursing a Major in Digital Media emphasis in New Media and minoring in Agricultural Business.

I grew up in Knoxville, Iowa on a small farm where we raise cattle and goats. I started showing cattle in third grade and started showing goats in seventh grade. From showing livestock, I have learned that with hard work and dedication anything is possible.

I have always been creative and fell in love with design through the livestock industry. Livestock producers' websites, logos, sale catalogs and livestock publications have always caught my attention and peaked my interest for design.

While at Northwest, I have taken multiple classes that have expanded my knowledge toward digital media. Within these classes, I have learned how to use programs such as: InDesign, Illustrator, Photoshop and Microsoft Excel. I have also learned how to code in HTML and CSS. I have experience designing webpages, creating advertisements, forming a media plan, and editing photos.
